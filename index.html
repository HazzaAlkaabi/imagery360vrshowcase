VR/360 Imagery Sharing Platforms

VeeR

Available on the Oculus Go and Rift and other several platforms. It is a community for sharing VR content.

As you can see here you could embed videos.

For Creators, upload VR videos/360 photos through their websiet even through their mobile apps.

There does not seem to be web-based tools to do edits like Roundme (Another platform, see further down in the page). Also, VeeR is a free platform.
